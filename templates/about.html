House Plant Hub

The House Plant Hub is a full-stack web application where users can create accounts, buy, sell, or trade house plants, and upload information about different house plants. This project was built for the OSU CS467 Online Capstone Course, Winter 2022.

Inspiration

With more people taking a passionate interest in raising houseplants, the House Plant Hub is an excellent resource for houseplant enthusiasts to find new plants and trade or sell plants they have carefully raised.
